🎓 What is KnowLedge?

KnowLedge helps you transform borrowed understanding into mastery. It tracks concepts you learn, guides you through focused clearing sessions, and marks each concept's status as On Loan, Clear, or Persists.

⚡

Scout Tagging

Paste your study notes or code. Scout automatically detects key concepts and logs them to your personal ledger.

🦉

Sage Clearing

Engage in a guided Socratic dialogue. Explain concepts in your own words and unlock mastery verification.

🔬

Lens Verification

Upload handwritten solutions to verify your reasoning and identify remaining knowledge gaps.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to master your learning with KnowLedge:

1 Add Learning Content

Start on My Ledger and paste your study material (notes, code snippets, formulas, or AI-generated explanations). Click Tag concepts to scan for key ideas.

  • Copy/paste text into the input area
  • Click "Tag concepts" button
  • Scout analyzes and extracts important ideas
2 Review Your Ledger

Your dashboard displays all tracked concepts with their current status:

  • On Loan — You haven't truly mastered this yet
  • Clear — You've demonstrated understanding
  • Persists — Concept needs further review

Start with concepts marked On Loan—these are your priority for mastery.

3 Clear with Sage

Click any concept to open a dialogue with Sage, an AI mentor. You'll explain the concept in your own words. Sage evaluates your response and either:

  • Confirms mastery — Concept updates to Clear ✓
  • Guides further — Ask follow-up questions to deepen understanding

This forces active learning instead of passive memorization.

4 Verify with Lens (Optional)

Use Lens to validate your understanding through multimodal feedback:

  • Upload a photo of handwritten solutions
  • Lens analyzes your reasoning visually
  • Identifies gaps and mistakes with feedback
  • Concept may move to Persists for revisiting
5 Track Your Progress

Monitor your learning journey with data-driven insights:

  • Progress — View trend charts and weekly concept movement
  • Reports — Instructors can see aggregate class learning patterns

Celebrate milestones as you clear concepts and build true mastery!

Frequently Asked Questions

Search or browse common questions below:

What does "On Loan" mean?

"On Loan" means you've pasted or referenced this concept, but you haven't yet proven you truly understand it. It's called "borrowed" understanding—you took it from a source without making it your own. Your mission is to clear it.

How does a concept become "Clear"?

A concept becomes "Clear" when you explain it in your own words during a Sage session and Sage confirms you've demonstrated mastery. This proves you've moved from passive understanding to owned knowledge.

What is "Persists" status?

"Persists" indicates a concept where gaps were found during Lens verification (handwritten solution analysis). It signals that despite claiming mastery, your logic may have holes. Revisit with Sage to strengthen understanding.

How does Sage work?

Sage is an offline AI mentor that guides you through a Socratic clearing process. Instead of giving answers, it asks probing questions to help you discover the concept yourself. This active learning method builds deeper understanding.

What is Lens and how do I use it?

Lens is a visual verification tool. You upload a photo of your handwritten solution or diagram, and it analyzes your reasoning using AI. It identifies gaps in logic with visual feedback and suggestions.

How does Scout tag concepts automatically?

When you paste content, Scout uses AI to identify key concepts and terms. It learns from your course materials and syllabus to recognize important ideas relevant to your studies.

What is My Ledger?

My Ledger is your personal learning dashboard. It displays all tracked concepts with their statuses, confidence levels, and timestamps. It's the central hub for managing your learning debt and tracking progress.

What is Solo Mode?

Solo Mode provides a focused, distraction-free environment for deep learning. You can practice clearing concepts without the full dashboard interface.

How do I view my progress?

Go to the Progress page to see:

  • Weekly trend charts showing concept movement
  • Statistics on cleared vs. pending concepts
  • Your learning velocity and growth over time
What about instructor Reports?

Reports provides instructors with aggregate class-level insights:

  • Which concepts are challenging for students
  • Class-wide progress trends
  • Recommended focus areas for instruction
Is my data private?

Yes. KnowLedge is designed to run entirely offline on your device. Your concept tracking, responses, and learning data never leave your computer. All processing happens locally.

How do I refresh my dashboard?

Use the Refresh Data button on My Ledger to sync the latest concept statuses. This fetches updated information from your local database.

I have more questions. How do I get help?

Use the floating help button (? in the bottom-right) to access this Help Center from any page. If you're stuck, check the FAQ section or revisit the Step-by-Step guide for your specific question.
