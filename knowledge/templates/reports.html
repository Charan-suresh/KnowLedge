How Gemma 4 Helps

Four AI agents, one Gemma model, zero private servers needed.

Scout

reads your study material and extracts the key concepts you need to understand — turning passive reading into an active list of knowledge gaps.

Sage

questions you Socratically. It never just gives you the answer. It asks focused, escalating questions until it's confident you genuinely understand — not just memorised — the concept.

Lens

analyses photos of your handwritten notes, detecting whether your reasoning holds up and flagging conceptual gaps before they become exam surprises.

Solo Mode

gives you a timed, open-ended recall challenge to prove you can explain a concept without any prompting — the gold standard of durable learning.

🔒
Privacy Guarantee — No student data is exposed here

This view shows aggregated concept data only. No student names. No conversation transcripts. No individual session details. Students' Socratic dialogues with Gemma 4 never leave their device — only concept names and mastery status are synced. All inference runs on-device via Gemma 4.

Why This Exists

A teacher with 35 students has ~3 minutes of individual time per student per week. When a student says they understand quadratic equations, there is no way to verify that — until the exam. KnowLedge surfaces which concepts are uncleared across the class, privately, so teachers know exactly where to intervene before it's too late.
