How Gemma 4 Helps

Four AI agents, one Gemma model, zero private servers needed.

Scout reads your study material and extracts the key concepts you need to understand — turning passive reading into an active list of knowledge gaps.

Sage questions you Socratically. It never just gives you the answer. It asks focused, escalating questions until it's confident you genuinely understand — not just memorised — the concept.

Lens analyses photos of your handwritten notes, detecting whether your reasoning holds up and flagging conceptual gaps before they become exam surprises.

Solo Mode gives you a timed, open-ended recall challenge to prove you can explain a concept without any prompting — the gold standard of durable learning.

All inference runs on Gemma 4 (unsloth/gemma-4-4b-it) via a Hugging Face Space backend — no paid API keys required. In local mode, it runs entirely on your laptop via Ollama.
